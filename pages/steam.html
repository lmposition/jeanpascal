Incredible!

Farcry 4 was my first open world so I'm not going to be very objective, clearly a lot of mechanics were taken from Far Cry 3, as Ubisoft does so well. But Far Cry 4 is for me the only Far Cry with such a feeling of freedom, the landscapes, the interactions with nature are so pleasant.

The lore is very interesting and the story entertaining. Rabi Ray Rana's humor is nice. Far Cry 4 was released in 2014 but the graphics are still as very very nice.

This is the second time I've finished it, I still have this little frustration of making this cornelian choice between Sabal and Amita even if I don't like either. Pagan is such an interesting antagonist because for once he's not 100% bad and that's what makes him so endearing.

I don't think it's a game that will please all players in 2024 but I can't recommend it enough.

9/10 Love it incredible experience that will stay with me for a long time
